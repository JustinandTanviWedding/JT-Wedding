Attire & Dress Code
Haldi
Wear yellow or orange — this is the one time getting messy is part of the fun. Turmeric paste gets everywhere, so don't wear anything you'd be sad to ruin. Light cotton kurtas, simple salwar suits, or even a plain yellow outfit works perfectly. Turmeric is considered sacred and purifying in Hindu tradition — it's believed to bless, cleanse, and beautify the couple before the union.

Mehndi
Colourful and festive — greens, pinks, turquoise, and bright prints are ideal. This is a relaxed, joyful event so think comfortable and vibrant.

Sangeet
This is the party night — think bold, festive, and fun. Lehengas, anarkalis, sharara sets, or a dressy Indo-Western outfit for women. Men can wear a stylish kurta with trousers or even a well-fitted blazer with Indian elements. Bright and celebratory colours encouraged — this is your chance to go all out.
