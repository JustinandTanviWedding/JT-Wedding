Before You Pack

What to Know

India is incredible — but genuinely unlike anywhere most Americans have traveled. A few things to sort before you leave.

🔌

Power adapter — India uses Type C/D/M plugs at 230V. Your US plugs won't fit. Pack a universal travel adapter before you leave.

🔋

Portable power bank — Four-day event with long outdoor hours means your phone will need a boost between ceremonies. Essential.

💳

Notify your bank before you leave. International card activity triggers fraud alerts. A quick call saves a lot of frustration at checkout.

💊

Basic medications — Pack antidiarrheals (Imodium), antacids, antihistamines, and any prescription meds. Indian pharmacies are excellent but knowing your generic names helps.

🌿

Insect repellent — A DEET-based repellent is recommended for evenings near the coast. Pack it from home; it's harder to find abroad.

☀️

Sunscreen & a hat — December is dry and sunny in coastal Karnataka. Beach ceremonies + outdoor events = real sun exposure. SPF 50+ strongly recommended.

👟

Comfortable sandals — You'll remove shoes at every religious space. Slip-ons or sandals that are easy on and off make life much easier across the weekend.

🌀

Embrace the chaos. Traffic, noise, sensory overload — all part of it. India moves at its own rhythm. Once you stop fighting it, it becomes one of the most electrifying places on earth.
