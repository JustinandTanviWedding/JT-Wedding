The Celebration

A Four-Day Wedding

We're so excited to celebrate with you as we bring together our families, friends, and traditions.

🌿
Mehendi

December 27, 2026
An intimate evening to begin our wedding celebrations. Guests are invited to decorate their arms with intricate henna designs in preparation for the celebration ahead.

💍
Muhurta

December 28, 2026
A sacred pre-wedding ritual rooted in coastal Karnataka traditions. Toe rings are placed on the bride's feet — a deeply personal act marking her transition into married life.

🎶
Sangeet

December 28, 2026
An evening of music, dance, and laughter as both families share performances, stories, and the pure joy of coming together.

🥥🪷
Haldi & Roce

December 29–30, 2026
A fragrant blend of turmeric, rose water, and coconut milk is applied to the couple by loved ones. Expect laughter, lighthearted games, and hands glowing in golden hues.

Wedding & Reception

December 30, 2026
The ceremony begins at 5:30 PM at the beachfront mandap, followed by a reception dinner.
